Tomato Meat Sauce
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1 sweet yellow onion, chopped
crushed garlic to taste
2 lbs ground meat
28 oz crushed tomatoes
6 oz tomato paste
2 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp worchestershire sauce
2 tbsp fish sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 cup wine (red or white)
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
salt to taste
red pepper flakes to taste

Instructions
~~~~~~~~~~~~
1. Set instant pot to saute on high.
2. Heat olive oil in instant pot.
3. Add onion and saute until soft and translucent.
4. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
5. Add grouond meat and cook until browned.
6. Turn instant pot off of saute.
7. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar,
   worchestershire sauce, fish sauce, soy sauce,
   wine, basil, salt, and red pepper flakes.
8. Stir to combine.
9. Close lid on instant pot and set it to pressure
   cook on high for 12 minutes.
   (make sure to seal lid)
10. Once timer goes off let the instant pot naturally
    depressurize fully before removing lid.
11. Turn instant pot to saute on low for 30 minutes,
    stirring sauce occasionally.
12. After reducing sauce add extra salt if necessary.
