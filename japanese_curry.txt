Japanese Curry
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1 tbsp olive oil
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 tsp ginger, grated or 1/2 tsp ground ginger
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 lb chicken thighs, diced
4 cups chicken stock
1 apple, grated 
63g honey(3 tbsp)
51g soy sauce (3 tbsp)
17g ketchup (1 tbsp)
2 carrots, chopped
1 lb yukon gold potatos, diced
8oz boxed japanese curry roux squares or homemade curry roux
salt to taste (if using homemade roux)

Curry Roux
~~~~~~~~~~
12g japanese curry powder (2 tbsp)
1.25oz olive oil (3.5 tbsp)
1.25oz flour (3.5 tbsp)


Instructions
~~~~~~~~~~~~
1. Heat olive oil in pot over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook till soft and translucent.
3. Add ginger and garlic and cook till fragrent.
4. Add chicken and brown till no pink showing.
5. Add stock, apple sauce, honey, soy sauce, ketchup and stir to combine.
6. Add carrots and potatoes.
7. Bring to a low simmer and cook for 15 minutes or until the potatoes can be easily pierced with a wooden skewer.
8. Turn off heat and stir in curry roux.
9. Add salt to taste.
10. Serve with desired portion size of white rice.
